# AlphaLoops

**Carrier intelligence for developers and go-to-market teams in transportation.**

AlphaLoops helps you understand which carriers to target, what technology they run, and how to prioritize outreach — with structured carrier intelligence delivered through API, SDK, CLI, and CRM integrations.

If you're building freight, insurance, fintech, compliance, or sales tooling for transportation, AlphaLoops gives you direct programmatic access to carrier profiles, fleet data, inspections, crash history, authority records, news, and contact intelligence.

https://runalphaloops.com/

## What AlphaLoops Is

AlphaLoops is a carrier intelligence platform built on top of FMCSA and proprietary enrichment layers.

According to the platform, AlphaLoops profiles carriers with:

- operational data
- technology signals
- growth indicators
- risk flags
- verified contacts

It also exposes API access to **2.3M+ carrier records** and **200+ data fields**.

## Why It Exists

Basic FMCSA records are useful for compliance, but limited for product and GTM workflows.

AlphaLoops is built for questions like:

- Which carriers run **Samsara**, **Motive**, or **Geotab**?
- Which fleets are growing or shrinking?
- Which carriers fit a specific operational profile?
- Which decision-makers are attached to a carrier?

The platform’s public positioning is clear: help teams stop “selling into trucking blind.”
